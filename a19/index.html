    Using Oxygenation as a Biomarker:

    Providing novel, critically important diagnostic and prognostic biomarkers for coronary artery disease AND microvascular disease.


    We have optimized an MRI protocol that can be executed at any clinical facility right now. Our protocol employs standard MRIs already in use at hospitals and requires no additional hardware or scanning software. It is compatible with all leading MRI systems (Siemens, GE, Philips) and includes the only software that can efficiently analyze this type of data.


    We offer a groundbreaking solution for cardiac MRIs, eliminating the need for needles and ensuring ultra-fast results. Our cutting-edge tool leverages advanced Glassbox AI technology to enable dynamic and high-resolution mapping of findings, including comprehensive coverage of coronary and microvascular diseases.

    Personalized recommendations:

    Area19 combines a person's oxygenation with standard clinical data to create individual cardiovascular health signature.



    Our signature encodes cardiac health status along with how the person's heart health will evolve over time.
