A little about me

I am currently studying Neuroscience at McGill University and doing research in Gil Bub's Lab as well as the CMR Research Group at the MHUC. My research focuses on cardiac disease and abnormalities, where I combine machine learning and modeling to study cadiac arrhymias. More recently, I have engaged in using machine learning for automated diagnostic classification and analysis in patients with heart disease using cardiac MRI images. I am also the president of academics at Youreka Montreal, where I lead and instruct a cohort of high-school, CEGEP and univeristy undergraduates through a research project.
